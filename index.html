Business Model

Approaches to meet our client’s needs

Time & Material What you’ll get

With time and material model, entities can get access to the highly skilled developers across the world and have to pay for the time that resources have spent on the particular project.

  • Dedicated desk
  • 8 hour usage of our team
  • Project manager support
  • Latest tools

Hire Team What you’ll get

Our clients can hire developers on hourly, part time, and full time basis as per the business preference. By opting for hiring developers’ models, customers can shorten development cycles and save infrastructure cost.

Customers can rely on us and hand over the project. In this model, we discuss on demand functionalities as well as cost in advance before starting with the project.
